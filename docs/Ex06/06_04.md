# Task 04: Contain and remediate

:::Architecture(team=Architecture)
#### Security Architecture Team  

1. If malicious emails continue to arrive, you can:  

    1. Go to **Email and collaboration** > **Explorer**.  
    1. Filter by **Subject**, **URL**, or **Sender address**.  
    1. Select an email on the table.
    1. Select **Take action** > Select **Move or delete** > **Soft delete** or **Hard delete** > **Submit**.  
    1. Go to **Investigation & response** > **Actions & submissions** > **Action center**.
    1. Check **Pending/History** to validate remediation results.  

1. If a device exhibits credential-theft behavior:  

    1. Go to **Assets** > **Devices**, then select the device.  
    1. Open the context menu (**...**) and select an action like **Isolate device** or **Run antivirus scan**.  
    1. Validate the action's success in **Action center** under **Pending/History**.  

:::

:::Engineering(team=Engineering)
#### Security Engineering and Administration  

{: .important } General information for the Engineering team.

1. Isolate a device:  

    1. Go to **Assets** > **Devices**, then select the device.  
    1. Select the context menu (**...**) > **Isolate device**.  

1. Quarantine a file:  

    1. Go to **Incidents & alerts** > **Incidents**, then select the **EICAR_Test_File** incident.  
    1. Select **Evidence and response** > **File** > **Quarantine** (or **Undo quarantine**).  

1. Purge email for impacted recipients:  

    1. Go to **Email and collaboration** > **Explorer**.  
    1. Select the email message.  
    1. Select **Take action** > **Move** or **Delete**.  

1. Revoke OAuth app consents or disable an app:  

    1. Go to **Cloud Apps** > **Cloud App catalog**.  
    1. Select the app and select **Sanction** or **Unsanction**.  

1. Safeguard affected users:  

    1. Go to `entra.microsoft.com`, select **Users**, then select the user.  
    1. Select **Revoke sessions**.  

:::

:::SOC(team=SOC)
#### SOC Analyst  

{: .important } General information for the SOC Analysts.

1. Queue and monitor actions:  

    1. Go to **Incidents**, select the incident, then select **Evidence and response**.  
    1. Select items (Device/File/Email/User/Cloud App) and queue necessary actions.  
    1. Open **Action center** and verify **Pending** or **History** actions.  

1. Add incident comments:  

    - In **Manage incident**, record the containment timeline. For example, *"10:24 NZDT - VM fully isolated; 10:29 - email soft-deleted org-wide; residual: monitor OAuth app re-consent."*

1. Resolve the incident once all actions are complete.
