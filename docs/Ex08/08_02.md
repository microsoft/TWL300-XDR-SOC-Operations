# Task 02: Calculate and review MTTD, MTTR, and first-action metrics

:::Architecture(team=Architecture)
#### Security Architecture Team  

1. Open the incident CSV you receive from the team to confirm these columns exist:  

    - Incident ID  
    - Severity  
    - First activity time  
    - Creation time  
    - First action time (analyst note)  
    - Last update time (proxy for resolution)  

1. Compute metrics:  

    - MTTD = Created − First activity  
    - First-Action = First action − Created  
    - MTTR = Last update − First activity  

1. Filter by **Severity** and calculate **average**, **median**, and **90th percentile**.  

1. Build an Excel or Power BI dashboard showing **SLA compliance** and **outliers**.  

1. Investigate incidents with breached SLAs.  
:::

:::Engineering(team=Engineering)
#### Security Engineering and Administration  

{: .note } Informational for what the Engineering team could do at this point.

1. Schedule a daily export job or perform a manual CSV download.  

1. Standardize timestamps to **UTC** or **local** consistently.  

1. Confirm **Last update time** populates correctly on status changes.  

1. Deliver a monthly summary to the Architecture team.  

:::

:::SOC(team=SOC)
#### SOC Analyst  

1. Export your assigned incidents weekly.  

1. Log your first-action and status changes.  

1. Note any delays and root causes. For example, *"Awaiting business approval."*  

1. Present individual SLA compliance in team review.
